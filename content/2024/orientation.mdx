## Calendar of events

The calendar below should display correctly in your local time; however, to check to see if this is true: &ldquo;Orientation Kickoff&rdquo; should be on Sunday, August 23rd, at 3:00PM EDT (UTC-4:00).

Clicking the events will open the [Guidebook](https://guidebook.com/guide/173503/) page for that event, where you can find more details. This page simply offers a more &ldquo;Google Calendar&rdquo;-like view of Orientation week.

To add all the events to your Google Calendar, click [here](https://calendar.google.com/calendar?cid=anE4ZmJtMXZzOW92b2k3Y2w4NGMwMWhlNDRAZ3JvdXAuY2FsZW5kYXIuZ29vZ2xlLmNvbQ). Credits to Dillon (Class of 2024)!
